About WIPA

WOMEN IN POWER AFRICA hereafter referred to as (WIPA) is a women empowerment platform established in 2018 to Build, Empower and Uplift African women to become great and impactful leaders in any fi eld they so choose that aligns to their God given purposes.

WIPA is registered NOP established for the purposes of BUILDING, EMPOWERING AND UPLIFTING African women around the globe through inspiration drawn from other SUCCESSFUL African female leaders so that through the success stories of these women others may be inspired and motivated to BELIEVE in the GREATNESS that is INBORN within them.
