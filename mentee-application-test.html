Mentorship Program:
We provide experienced African female mentors from different fields who coach mentees with wisdom acquired from their own experiences. The mentors provide mentees with insight from their own journeys so that the mentees success journey may be accelerated. Annual Mentorship workshop- This is facilitated by a qualified coach.
Purpose
Purpose is to introduce new mentors and mentees to the mentorship program and create a road map of the year ahead . Mentorship program. Train mentees on what is mentorship and how it works.
