Nceba Mathabatha
Founder of Women In Power Africa & CEO of TaxPro-SA
Ncebakazi is qualified CA(SA) and a registered tax practitioner with a BAccScie degree from WITS, Honours from UNISA and MCOM TAXATION from UP. She is the founder of Women In Power Africa and CEO of TaxPro- SA a tax consulting boutique firm. She has gained her professional experience in finance, accounting, taxation, auditing and commercial management from the various Snr management roles she’s held in the various companies she has worked for.

Ncebakazi continues to work hard to change her story and she is now an influential, notable and relevant speaker who addresses burning issues amongst women with wisdom. She lives to encourage women to rise above their present limitations and find their purpose in life, achieve that purpose through the God given greatness that is within them.
Besa Ruele
CEO of Hollard Life Solutions
Besa Ruele, the CEO of Hollard Life Solutions. A Fellow of the Actuarial Society of South Africa and holds BSc in Mathematics from the University of Botswana. Her career in the financial industry spans over 20 years in consulting and insurance roles. Prior to joining the Hollard Group, she served in various roles in actuarial science and management. Ruele joined the Hollard Insurance Group in 2018 and has been at the helm of the Life Business unit.

Ncebakazi Msomi
VP Business ABSA Support & Recording Artist
She is a business turnaround strategist, with years of industry wide experience in Credit Risk Evaluations, Monitoring and Management. With a BComm Economics and Supply Chain Management degree, an MBA and qualifications in theology, she is doing further research into microfinance and SMMEs. Portia Mngomezulu
CEO of Portia M
Portia continues to take big strikes towards achieving her vision of Making the Portia M brand of products a leading skin care brand in Africa and the world at large.

She has been featured on platforms such as the SABC (radio and television), eTV, CNBC, Bona, City Press, Truelove Mag, Destiney. FNB, Mojalove and Drum. The award winning entrepreneur has left audiences inspired when telling the Portia M story across Africa, Europe and Asia.
Nozibele Mayaba
Social Entrepreneur & Published Author
Nozibele Qamgana-Mayaba is an award winning YouTuber, HIV activist, self-published author, TV host and podcaster. Her first book “I am still me” , based on her life living positively with HIV, has sold more than 5000 copies since 2020. She recently partnered with Honey TV on a brand new show called #YesIHaveHIV. She has also collaborated with a German broadcaster, Deutsche Welle, to launch the #DontHoldBack podcast.
