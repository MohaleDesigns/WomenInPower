Our Mentorship Board

Nceba Mathabatha

Founder of Women In Power Africa & CEO of TaxPro-SA

Ncebakazi is qualified CA(SA) and a registered tax practitioner with a BAccScie degree from WITS, Honours from UNISA and MCOM TAXATION from UP. She is the founder of Women In Power Africa and CEO of TaxPro- SA a tax consulting boutique firm. She has gained her professional experience in finance, accounting, taxation, auditing and commercial management from the various Snr management roles she’s held in the various companies she has worked for.

Ncebakazi continues to work hard to change her story and she is now an influential, notable and relevant speaker who addresses burning issues amongst women with wisdom. She lives to encourage women to rise above their present limitations and find their purpose in life, achieve that purpose through the God given greatness that is within them.

Zanele Bafo

Project co-ordinator Women In Power

Zanele is an experienced business developer and a marketing professional with over 5 years of professional work experience. She is well skilled at connecting professionals of different races from across the globe and engage them in an empowering and benevolent manner. She's able to take lead, motivate and mobilise different groups of people towards a common vision. She does motivational speaking in women gatherings/seminars.

Kwakhanya Mothata

Admitted attorney of the high court

Kwakhanya is a dedicated and driven legal professional, who upholds high standards of ethics and integrity in the practice of law. Born and bred in Kimberley and graduated from WITS University (LLB degree – 2012) and commenced articles in 2013 at Matthew and Partners. She worked her way up in the firm from occupying the position of a candidate attorney, obtaining her admission as an attorney and further advancing to occupy the position of Senior Associate, managing the firm’s Kimberley branch. She has also worked as a Legal Advisor at GWK, a diverse agricultural company in South Africa.

Kwakhanya is highly experienced in the following legal fields: litigation (Supreme Courts and Lower Courts), labour law (internal and external labour dispute mechanisms), criminal law, alternative dispute resolution mechanisms, property law, estate law, corporate and commercial law, compliance and company secretary (legal) duties and responsibilities. Kwakhanya prides herself in her professional demeanour, conscientiousness and commitment to excellence.

Fikile Elsie Simelane

Supply Chain Management expert

Fikile is a professional career woman who loves God. She is highly experienced in supply chain management with over 12 years of work experience in different organisations and government entities. She holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree from UNISA and various SCM and business management qualifications from various learning institutions. She is also a minister of the Gospel of Jesus Chris and is passionate about ministering and bringing hope and encouragement to women and youth. She ministers in South Africa and neighbouring countries. An experienced entrepreneur, Fikile owns a farming business in Swaziland, runs a an imports and manufacturing business in South Africa.

Fikile is passionate about developing and mentoring the people she leads. Her professional growth is illustrative of her continuous learning and development philosophy.

Vuyokazi

Admitted attorney and Notary Public of the High Court of South Africa

Vuyokazi holds a B Juris and an LLB Honours Law Degree from the University of the Western Cape. She is an admitted attorney and Notary Public of the High Court of South Africa. She practised as an attorney at Sonnenberg Hoffman Galombik in Cape Town, specialising in property, insurance litigation, commercial litigation and general commercial matters.

Vuyokazi thereafter worked for FNB Corporate where she gained experience in various departments. Through FNB Corporate she earned her experience in international trade financing, debt restructuring, tailoring various finance products, property finance as well as business development and client portfolio management. Vuyokazi joined Billion as Legal Counsel and is now a full-time Director at Billion Group. She is now responsible for various property developments within Billion.
