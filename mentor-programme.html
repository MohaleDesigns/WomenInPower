WIPA 2022 Leadership Seminar: Take Up Space

Event Details

About

To Empower African Women to take up leadership roles in any field they choose and take up space in those fields and lead effectively. Our Key note speakers will share on their own leadership journeys. They will dive deep into the challenges they faced as African women and how they overcame those. These powerful leaders will also be giving nuggets of how to become an effective female African leader that is well respected in the industry.

Ultimately, this event is purposed at inspiring African women to believe in themselves and their God given ability to lead effectively and to dominate by taking up space in any sphere they so choose.

Vision

Turning FOLLOWERS into LEADERS and LEADERS into AGENTS of change. Connecting African Women of every background, ethnicity and age to their God ordained destinies.
